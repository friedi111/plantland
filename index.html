Plant Care 101

Everything you need to keep your leafy friends thriving.

☀️

Perfect Light

Most house plants love bright, indirect light. Avoid harsh direct sun which can scorch leaves, but don't keep them in the dark!

💧

The Finger Test

Overwatering is the #1 plant killer. Stick your finger 2 inches into the soil; if it feels dry, it's time to give them a drink.

☁️

Stay Humid

Tropical plants (like Monsteras) love moisture. Mist them regularly or group them together to create a mini jungle microclimate.

🧼

Clean Leaves

Dust blocks sunlight! Wipe leaves with a damp cloth every few weeks so your plants can breathe and photosynthesize properly.
