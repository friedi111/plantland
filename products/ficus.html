Ficus Lyrata

🎻 Fiddle Leaf 💧 Regular Water ☀️ Bright Light

The Ficus Lyrata — better known as the Fiddle Leaf Fig — is the darling of interior designers worldwide. With its large, violin-shaped leaves and tall sculptural form, it commands attention in any room. Originally from West African tropical rainforests, this tree brings drama and elegance to modern spaces.

Size

Large — 60–90 cm tall

Difficulty

Moderate — Needs consistency

Growth

Moderate — with the right conditions

Pet Safe?

No — Toxic to cats & dogs

Care Tips

  • Light: Bright, indirect light is essential. Rotate weekly for even growth.
  • Water: Water when the top 3 cm of soil is dry. Consistent schedule is key.
  • Humidity: Prefers moderate to high humidity. Wipe leaves to remove dust.
  • Temperature: 18–25°C. Avoid sudden temperature changes and drafts.
  • Feeding: Monthly liquid fertilizer during the growing season (spring–autumn).
