Monstera Deliciosa

$45

🌿 Tropical 💧 Moderate Water ☀️ Indirect Light

The Monstera Deliciosa — also known as the Swiss Cheese Plant — is the ultimate statement piece for any room. Its iconic split leaves bring a bold, tropical energy that instantly transforms your space. Native to Central American rainforests, this climber is as resilient as it is beautiful.

Size

Medium — 45–60 cm tall

Difficulty

Easy — Perfect for beginners

Growth

Fast grower in warm conditions

Pet Safe?

No — Toxic to cats & dogs

Care Tips

  • Light: Bright, indirect light. Can tolerate some shade but will grow slower.
  • Water: Water when the top 5 cm of soil feels dry. Roughly once a week.
  • Humidity: Loves humidity — mist regularly or place near a humidifier.
  • Temperature: 18–30°C. Keep away from cold drafts.
  • Feeding: Fertilize monthly during spring and summer with liquid plant food.
