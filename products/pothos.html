Golden Pothos

$25

🌱 Trailing Vine 💧 Low Water 🌑 Any Light

The Golden Pothos (Epipremnum aureum) is one of the most beloved houseplants on Earth — and for good reason. Its cascading, heart-shaped leaves with golden variegation look stunning on shelves, hanging planters, or trailing across a bookcase. Practically unkillable, it's the perfect plant for first-timers.

Size

Trailing — 30–60 cm vines

Difficulty

Very Easy — Thrives on neglect

Growth

Fast — Can grow 30 cm/month

Pet Safe?

No — Toxic if chewed

Care Tips

  • Light: Low to bright indirect light. More light = more variegation.
  • Water: Let soil dry between waterings. Droopy leaves = thirsty plant.
  • Humidity: Not fussy. Normal room humidity is perfectly fine.
  • Temperature: 15–30°C. Keep away from cold windows in winter.
  • Feeding: Light feeding once a month in spring and summer.
