Snake Plant

$30

🏜️ Drought Tolerant 💧 Low Water 🌑 Low Light OK

The Snake Plant (Sansevieria trifasciata) is the ultimate low-maintenance companion. Its striking, sword-like leaves with bold yellow-green edges make a modern architectural statement. A NASA-approved air purifier, it converts CO₂ to oxygen even at night — perfect for bedrooms.

Size

Medium — 30–50 cm tall

Difficulty

Very Easy — Nearly indestructible

Growth

Slow and steady

Pet Safe?

No — Mildly toxic if ingested

Care Tips

  • Light: Tolerates low light to bright indirect. Avoid direct afternoon sun.
  • Water: Let soil dry out completely between waterings. Every 2–3 weeks.
  • Humidity: No special requirements — thrives in normal room humidity.
  • Temperature: 15–30°C. Very adaptable but avoid frost.
  • Feeding: Feed sparingly — once in spring and once in summer is enough.
