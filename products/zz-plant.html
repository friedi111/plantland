ZZ Plant

$35

✨ Glossy Leaves 💧 Very Low Water 🌑 Low Light OK

The ZZ Plant (Zamioculcas zamiifolia) is a modern classic with glossy, dark-green leaves arranged in graceful, arching stems. It stores water in its thick rhizomes, making it one of the most drought-tolerant houseplants you can find. Sleek, architectural, and practically impossible to kill — it's the plant for people who forget to water.

Size

Medium — 40–60 cm tall

Difficulty

Very Easy — Thrives on neglect

Growth

Slow — Patient and steady

Pet Safe?

No — Toxic if ingested

Care Tips

  • Light: Tolerates low light. Grows faster in bright, indirect light.
  • Water: Very drought tolerant. Water only every 2–4 weeks.
  • Humidity: No special needs. Standard room humidity is perfect.
  • Temperature: 15–28°C. Average room temperatures are ideal.
  • Feeding: Feed 2–3 times per year with diluted liquid fertilizer.
